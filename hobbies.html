Gaming

Being a full time student, and having a part time job does not give you very much free time. However, when I am able to sit down and enjoy some free time, gaming is my favorite hobby. I can spend hours on my favorite FPS without getting bored.

Valorant

Valorant is my favorite FPS of all time! I have quite a few hours played in this game, and in my peek I was Immortal rank. My favorite agent is Jett because she is an aggresive character and it leads to the most fun.

Overwatch

I did not start playing Overwatch until Overwatch 2 came out but I had instant regret about not having played it earlier. Unfortunately, after quite a few hours into the game I started to realize why the game was so hated. I still hop on the game from time to time as it is one of the best paced FPS ever made.
